THE RESORT / Privacy Policy

Important — Please read before you book

The following terms and conditions apply to all bookings and stays at Dhauladhar Heights Resort. For questions, reach us at +91 80915-08200, +91 70188-41900 or reservation@dhauladharheightsresort.com

  • Payment & Booking
  • Identity Proof
  • Cancellation Policy
  • Child & Extra Bed Policy
  • Visitor Policy
  • Other Important Rules

    • Booking & Payment

  • Room bookings are subject to prior confirmation by email or phone.
  • Payment has to be cleared before departure.
  • Payment can be made through Master/Visa Card, IMPS/NEFT, QR scan code and by cash as well.
  • No refund will be given for short stay or any un-availed services.

    • As per Government rules, it is mandatory for all guests (adult/child) to show valid ID proof at the time of arrival. Accepted ID proofs: Aadhaar Card, Voter Card, Driving License, Passport.

  • PAN Card will not be accepted as a valid ID proof.
  • Foreign nationals are required to present a valid passport & visa.
  • The resort reserves the right to disallow/refuse check-in if a guest does not have valid ID proof as stated above. Right of admission is reserved.
  • Guest must share company/GST details prior to arrival in case GST billing is required. Company details cannot be amended post check-in.

    • Cancellation Policy

  • 100% cancellation charges will be levied if cancelled after check-in date or in case of no-show.
  • 100% cancellation charges will be levied if cancelled within 7 days before check-in date.
  • Free cancellation if cancelled 7 or more days before check-in date.

    • Child & Extra Bed Policy

  • Child below 5 years of age without extra bed will not be charged.
  • Child between 5–11 years of age without extra bed will be charged as per the meal plan only.
  • Child/person above 11 years of age will be charged as an adult.

    • In order to maintain the privacy of guests and the tranquility of the resort, visitors are generally allowed to meet guests in public areas during the day (08:00 AM to 07:00 PM). Visitors are not allowed to access guest rooms.

  • The visitor will have to show a valid address proof as per resort policy.
  • The resort reserves the right to refuse visitor access in order to protect guest privacy and safety.
  • Effective 2nd October 2008 the Government introduced "No Smoking" legislation for hotels, restaurants and all public places. Smoking is prohibited in all parts of the resort.
  • Any change in taxes levied by the Government will be charged extra and collected at the time of billing.
  • Pets are not allowed in the resort premises.
  • Arms and ammunition are strictly not allowed in the resort premises.
